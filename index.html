ABOUT THE ARCHITECT

Henok Akirso is an Embedded Systems and Security Engineer with a BSc in Computer Science from Ambo University, focused on building intelligent, secure and scalable digital systems. My work spans low-level programming, cybersecurity and AI-driven architecture with hands-on experience in C, Python and embedded hardware. I approach engineering with precision and a system-level mindset designing solutions that are reliable, adaptable and built to last.

Alongside my technical work, I’ve been teaching Mathematics and Spoken English in Hidasie Academy since last year. Helping students build strong analytical and communication skills. I’m deeply passionate about coding, algorithmic thinking and system architecture. I actively pursue work that aligns with these interests.

Driven by a long-term vision for Africa’s technological growth, I aim to contribute to building resilient digital infrastructure that supports innovation, security and self-reliance. Helping shape a future where Africa develops its own world-class technologies.
